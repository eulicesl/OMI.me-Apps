Brain Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/12/2024

Introduction

Welcome to Brain. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Information We Collect

  • User Identifier (UID): A unique identifier used to associate your data within the system.
  • Memory Data: Information you input into the system that forms your memory graph.
  • Statistics: Anonomous usage data to help us improve the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Brain service
  • To create and manage your personal memory graph
  • To improve and optimize our service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and processed in the following ways:

  • Memory Graph Data: Stored in a PostgreSQL database with relationships and metadata
  • Chat History: Not permanently stored - only used during active sessions
  • Local Storage: Some preferences and session data stored in your browser

While we implement security measures like rate limiting and encryption, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time through the profile menu, which will permanently remove:

  • All memory nodes and relationships
  • Your user profile and preferences
  • Discord linking information
  • Any locally stored data

Disclaimer of Responsibility

Please note:

  • We are not responsible for any decisions made based on information stored in or generated by the system
  • The accuracy and reliability of memory connections and AI-generated responses cannot be guaranteed
  • We are not liable for any data breaches or unauthorized access despite our security measures
  • The service is provided "as is" without any warranties of any kind
  • Users are responsible for the accuracy and legality of the information they input

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time without notice.
