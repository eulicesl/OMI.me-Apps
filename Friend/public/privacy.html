Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect and store the following information:

  • Conversation transcripts
  • User preferences and settings
  • Usage analytics
  • Personality traits and customizations

Data Usage

The collected data is used to:

  • Provide personalized AI responses
  • Improve user experience
  • Generate analytics and insights
  • Maintain conversation context

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database and is not shared with third parties. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can access your data through the dashboard
  • You can modify your settings and preferences at any time
  • You can delete all your data permanently through the settings page
  • Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or data practices, please contact us.
